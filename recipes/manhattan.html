Manhattan

Description

Ingredients

  • Bourbon of choice
  • Sweet Vermouth
  • Amaro, not necessary
  • Angostura Bitters
  • Luxardo Cherry

Steps

  1. Pour equal parts bourbon and sweet vermouth (If using Amaro, pour equal equal amount of Amaro, as well)
  2. 3 dashes of bitters
  3. Add Luxardo cherry
