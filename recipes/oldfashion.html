Old Fashion

Description

Ingredients

  • Orange bitters
  • Angostura bitters
  • Bourbon of choice
  • Simple syrup
  • Luxardo cherry
  • Orange

Steps

  1. 2 dashes each of orange bitters and Angostura bitters
  2. .5 - 1 ounce of simple syrup
  3. Stir, add ice
  4. Pour 2 ounces of bourbon, and stir
  5. Add orange peel, and luxardo cherry
